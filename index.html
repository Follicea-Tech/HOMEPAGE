About Us

Science Rooted in Purpose

Founded in Kyoto, Follicea Technologies is a biotech startup dedicated to developing innovative drug therapies for infertility. We believe every person deserves access to effective, science-backed treatment options.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in reproductive medicine, molecular biology, and drug development. Working from our base in Kyoto — a city at the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern innovation — we are developing targeted therapies designed to address the root causes of infertility rather than its symptoms alone.

We hold the belief that the path to parenthood should be accessible to all, and our science is driven by that commitment.

Our Scientific Approach

We develop precision drug candidates targeting the biological mechanisms underlying infertility, with a focus on efficacy, safety, and patient quality of life.

Targeted Drug Discovery

Identifying and validating novel molecular targets in reproductive function using high-throughput screening and computational modeling.

Small Molecule Therapeutics

Designing orally bioavailable small molecules with favorable pharmacokinetic profiles for accessible, patient-friendly treatment.

Translational Research

Bridging bench science and clinical reality through rigorous preclinical studies and collaboration with leading reproductive medicine centers.
