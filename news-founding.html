Announcement

Follicea Technologies Established in Kyoto

📅 April 2025 🏢 Corporate Announcement 📍 Kyoto, Japan

We are proud to announce the official founding of Follicea Technologies, Inc. — a new clinical-stage biotech startup based in Kyoto, Japan, dedicated to developing innovative pharmacological treatments for infertility. Today marks the beginning of a mission-driven journey to change how reproductive medicine approaches one of the most personal and underserved challenges in modern healthcare.

Who We Are

Follicea Technologies was founded by a multidisciplinary team with deep expertise spanning reproductive biology, medicinal chemistry, drug development, and clinical medicine. Our founders have spent years at the intersection of academic research and pharmaceutical development, and have come together around a shared conviction: that people who struggle with infertility deserve better therapeutic options than those currently available.

Our name, Follicea, draws from the word "follicle" — the microscopic structure in the ovary from which eggs develop. It represents the biological focus of our work, and the hope that drives it.

We are building a company where every scientific decision is guided by a single question: will this genuinely help someone who is trying to become a parent?

— Founding Team, Follicea Technologies

Why Kyoto?

Kyoto is more than a beautiful city — it is a global hub of scientific excellence, home to one of Japan's most distinguished research universities and a thriving life sciences ecosystem. We chose Kyoto as our base for its extraordinary concentration of academic talent, its culture of careful, rigorous inquiry, and its proximity to world-class clinical reproductive medicine centers.

Japan also faces a unique demographic context. With one of the lowest birth rates among developed nations and a healthcare system that has historically underinvested in reproductive medicine therapeutics, the need for effective, accessible infertility treatments is acute. We are proud to begin our work here, with the ambition to expand its impact globally.

The Problem We Are Solving

Infertility affects approximately 1 in 6 reproductive-age adults worldwide — over 180 million people. Despite its prevalence, the pharmacological toolkit available to clinicians remains surprisingly limited. The dominant approach — hormonal stimulation via injectable gonadotropins — has changed little in decades. It is effective for some patients, but it is invasive, expensive, and fails many others entirely.

The fundamental problem is that most existing treatments work around the root causes of infertility rather than addressing them directly. At Follicea, we are taking a different approach: using molecular biology to identify the precise mechanisms that go wrong in infertility, then developing targeted small molecule drugs that intervene at those specific points.

Our goal: Develop orally available, mechanism-targeted therapeutics for infertility that offer better efficacy, improved tolerability, and greater accessibility than current treatments — designed with patients, not just biology, in mind.

What Comes Next

In the weeks and months ahead, our scientific team will be launching our lead target identification program, initiating our first academic research collaborations, and building the foundations of our drug discovery platform. We will share updates here as our work progresses.

  • Launch of lead target identification program (Q1 2025)
  • Research collaboration agreement with Kyoto University (Q1 2025)
  • Hit generation and early SAR campaign (planned H2 2025)
  • Lead optimization initiation (planned 2026)

About Follicea Technologies

Follicea Technologies, Inc. is a biotech startup based in Kyoto, Japan, focused on the discovery and development of small molecule therapeutics for infertility. The company is currently in the drug discovery stage, with a lead program targeting a novel pathway in follicular development. For more information, visit our website or contact us at info@folliceatech.com.
