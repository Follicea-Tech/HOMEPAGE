About Us

Science Rooted in Purpose

Founded in Kyoto, Follicea Technologies is a biotech startup dedicated to developing innovative drug therapies for infertility. We believe every person deserves access to effective, science-backed treatment options.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in reproductive medicine, molecular biology, and drug development. Working from our base in Kyoto — a city at the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern innovation — we are developing targeted therapies designed to address the root causes of infertility rather than its symptoms alone.

We hold the belief that the path to parenthood should be accessible to all, and our science is driven by that commitment.

Technology

A Platform Built to Move Fast

Our integrated drug discovery platform closes the loop between computation and experiment — virtual screening, automated synthesis, robotic assays, and organoid validation working together to identify clinical candidates at unprecedented speed.

In Silico Drug Design

Molecular docking and virtual HTS evaluate hundreds of millions of compounds — with AI predicting binding affinity and ADMET properties before any synthesis begins.

Automated Synthesis & Screening

Up to 18,000 peptide variants per month from our automated synthesis platform feed directly into 24/7 robotic assays — generating 100,000s of data points daily.

Organoid Validation

Patient-derived 3D tissue models validate drug candidates with 88–100% clinical prediction accuracy — bridging the gap between in vitro hits and in vivo outcomes.
