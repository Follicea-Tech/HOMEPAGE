Selected for the Nakanoshima Qross Drug Discovery Cluster Campus Program, 1st Cohort

📅 August 2025 🏆 Award / Selection

Follicea Technologies has been selected as a first-cohort supported startup for the "Drug Discovery and Commercialization Promotion Support Program" operated by Nakanoshima Qross, Osaka City's hub for future medical innovation. This initiative has been certified as part of the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare's "Drug Discovery Cluster Campus Development Project," aiming to provide comprehensive, end-to-end support for the commercialization of drug discovery seeds held by startups and academia, with the goal of strengthening Japan's innovative drug discovery capabilities.
