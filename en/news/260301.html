Selected for the Kansai Startup Incubation Program "Kidou", 4th Cohort

📅 March 2026 💰 Funding / Award

"Kidou" is an incubation program supported by a Kansai industry-academia-government collaboration to foster the creation of next-generation startups. In the 4th cohort, 5 companies were selected from more than 100 applicants. Selected companies receive funding of up to 10 million yen and approximately 6 months of support including expert mentoring.
