Follicea Technologies Receives Merit Award at Kansai Mirai Venture Award "Mirai Way"

📅 June 2026 🏆 Award

Follicea Technologies was selected as a finalist in the 3rd Kansai Mirai Venture Award "Mirai Way," hosted by Kansai Mirai Bank, and received a Merit Award following the final pitch event held on June 18, 2026.

The award selected 10 finalists from more than 100 applicants. Follicea presented its business plan, "Reducing uncertainty in the birth of life through a novel infertility treatment designed to reduce repeated IVF," and was recognized as one of six Merit Award winners.

"Mirai Way" is a program that identifies venture companies taking on innovative technologies and business models, and supports their business growth. This recognition further encourages Follicea Technologies to advance its research, development, and commercialization efforts toward creating new options in fertility care.
