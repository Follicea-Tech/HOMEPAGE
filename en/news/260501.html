Follicea Technologies Founded in Osaka

📅 May 2026 🏢 Corporate Announcement

We are pleased to announce the founding of Follicea Technologies, Inc. — a Kansai-based biotech startup specializing in the development of innovative pharmacological treatments for infertility. Today marks the beginning of a mission-driven journey: to reduce uncertainty in the birth of new life.

Who We Are

Follicea Technologies was founded by an interdisciplinary team with deep expertise spanning medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutical development, and stem cell biology. Our founding members bring years of experience at the intersection of academic research and pharmaceutical development, and came together with a shared purpose: to provide new treatment options for those struggling with infertility.

Scientific achievements from academia only create real value when translated to society. Contributing to the global challenge of infertility treatment is a benefit to all of humanity.

— Founding Team, Follicea Technologies
